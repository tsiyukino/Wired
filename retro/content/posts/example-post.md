## Images & Video

Standard Markdown image (from any URL or relative path):

![a description of the image](https://example.com/photo.jpg)

With an optional caption:

![alt text](https://example.com/photo.jpg "This appears below the image")

Self-hosted video file (MP4 or WebM):

@[video](https://example.com/clip.mp4)

YouTube embed (use just the video ID, not the full URL):

@[youtube](dQw4w9WgXcQ)

Links: [link text](https://example.com)
